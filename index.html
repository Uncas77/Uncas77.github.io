What is fast fashion?

Fast fashion is a new phenomenon which refers to the exploitation of humans through labor and the harm of animals.

What can we do to stop this?

  1. Buying used clothing
  2. Locally shoppiong
  3. Reduce of cloth consumption
  4. Educating yourself on sustainable fashion
  5. Supporting charity shops

Conclusion/So What

This idea has been surfacing the internet for the past couple years. By ignoring and supporting it, we are actively contributing to the problem. By partaking in these solutions,

we can solve the reccuring issue of fast fashion once and for all, ultimately benefitting the fashion industry.
